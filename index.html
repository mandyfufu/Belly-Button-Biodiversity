This page provides the data visualization of belly button bacteria cultures collected from a cohort 150+ test subject along with their demographic data. The purpose of this study is test the hypothesis that certain bacterias produce in the belly button can synthesize protein to taste like beef for "Implausible Beef". The goal is to sustainably manufacture a product that taste like beef.
